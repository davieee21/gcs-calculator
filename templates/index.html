🧠 GCS Score Calculator

The Glasgow Coma Scale (GCS) is a clinical tool used to assess consciousness in patients with head injury, ICU conditions, or in emergency settings. It helps guide urgent clinical decision-making and monitor neurological status.

This calculator helps estimate the GCS score and provides guidance on what the result may mean. Of great importance, the trend matters more than a single score. A declining score indicates worsening neurological status and requires urgent medical attention.
